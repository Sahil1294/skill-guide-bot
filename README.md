## 📌 What is Skill Guide?

**Skill Guide** is an intelligent AI chatbot built to help students, freshers, and professionals navigate their career journey. Whether you need feedback on your resume, want to prep for a job interview, or are looking to understand what skills you need — Skill Guide has you covered.

> No sign-up. No forms. Just chat.

---

## ✨ Features

When you open Skill Guide, you're greeted with **4 powerful modes** to choose from:

---

### 📄 1. Resume Feedback
> *Paste your resume. Get real feedback.*

- You share your resume content in text format
- The bot analyzes it for structure, clarity, impact, and ATS compatibility
- Receive actionable suggestions to make your resume stand out to recruiters

---

### 🎯 2. Interview Preparation
> *Tell us the role. We'll prepare you.*

- You specify the job role you're targeting (e.g. Data Analyst, Frontend Developer, Product Manager)
- Skill Guide generates a curated set of **role-specific interview questions**
- Covers technical, behavioral, and situational questions based on the role

---

### 🧩 3. Skill Gap Analysis
> *Know where you stand. Know what to learn.*

- You tell the bot the role you're aiming for
- Share your current skill set
- Skill Guide maps out the **gap between where you are and where you need to be**
- Get a clear roadmap of skills, tools, and resources to focus on

---

### 🎤 4. Mock Interview
> *Practice like it's real. Get scored.*

- You select the role you want to be interviewed for
- The bot conducts a **live simulated interview** — asking questions one by one
- Based on your answers, you receive:
  - ✅ Detailed feedback per answer
  - 📊 An overall performance score
  - 💡 Tips to improve weak areas
